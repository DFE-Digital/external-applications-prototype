Transfer academies to another academy trust

What this application is for

This application is for single and multi-academy trusts (SATs and MATs) wanting to transfer academies to another trust.

This includes:

  • merging a SAT with a MAT
  • moving academies from a MAT into another MAT
  • merging a MAT with another MAT

What this application is not for

This application is not for:

  • MATs wanting to add schools that are not academies
  • groups of local authority-maintained schools forming a MAT

You will first need to apply to convert maintained schools into academies.

What happens after you apply

We will:

  • send you a confirmation email, with a reference number for your application
  • contact you if we need any more information about the transfer
  • review your application, and a Regional Director will make a decision on it

If you have any questions about your application, contact the academies team:
