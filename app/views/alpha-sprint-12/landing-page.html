Transfer academies to another trust

What this service is for

This service is for single and multi-academy trusts (SATs and MATs) wanting to transfer academies to another trust.

This includes:

  • merging a SAT with a MAT
  • moving academies from a MAT into another MAT
  • merging a MAT with another MAT

What this service is not for

This service is not for:

  • MATs wanting to add schools that are not academies
  • groups of maintained schools forming a MAT

You will first need to apply to convert the schools into academies.
